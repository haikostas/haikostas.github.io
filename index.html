Total Reach is a personal dashboard that gathers the view counts from your own social media accounts and shows the running total on a single display.

YouTube Instagram Facebook TikTok Pinterest

How it works

You connect each of your own accounts through their official APIs. Total Reach reads only your own statistics — the view counts on the content you posted — and adds them into a single figure.

It does not access anyone else's data, never posts or changes anything, and stores its data locally on your own device. It's a private counter for your own numbers, not a tool that touches other people's accounts.
