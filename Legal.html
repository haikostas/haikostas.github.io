Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Total Reach ("the app") is a personal dashboard, operated by its individual owner, that displays the owner's own social media view-count statistics on a private screen. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

What data the app accesses

When you sign in with TikTok, the app requests only:

  • Basic profile information (user.info.basic): your account's open ID, display name, and avatar — used only to confirm which account is authorized.
  • Your video list (video.list): metadata for your own public videos, specifically each video's view count. These counts are added together to display a single total-views number.

The app accesses only the account that authorizes it. It does not access any other user's data, and it cannot post, modify, or delete content.

How data is used and stored

View counts are aggregated into a single total and shown on the owner's own display. The access token and the most recent totals are stored locally on the owner's own device. No data is transmitted to any third party, sold, shared, or used for advertising or profiling.

Data retention and deletion

Locally stored data can be deleted at any time by removing the app's local files or by revoking the app's access from your TikTok account settings.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact the app owner at the email address provided in the TikTok developer application.
